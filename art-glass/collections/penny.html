Penelope Wurr Glass

Penelope Wurr's design influences range from William Morris to Beatrix Potter to just a fascination with color and pattern combinations. "When I was growing up in London," says the artist, "I was surrounded by a mixture of modern architecture and fascinating wallpapers. I've been trying to bring them both back into fashion for years." Adapting some of the techniques of printmaking, Penelope developed a unique method of enameling on glass, which is patented in England and the U.S. Her designs are produced in limited editions.

Jules Cheret, French, (1836-1932)
Condition A
Original lithograph from the "Les Maitres de l'Affiche" series
Printed by Imprimerie Chaix, Paris, 1898
11 3/8 in x 15 3/4 in


Subscribers to the "Les Maitres de l'Affiche" series received four special plates annually. These premium original lithographs, each an original design, were created specifically for this series, and are not seen in any other format, unlike the regular plates, which are reduced versions of larger posters.

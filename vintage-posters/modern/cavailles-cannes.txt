The Lights of Cannes
By Jules Cavailles, French, (1901-1977)
Circa 1967
21 1/2 in x  29 3/4 in
$100
Cavailles was part of the group known as “Painters of the Poetic Reality” (La réalité poétique). His work is represented in many leading collections and museums, including the Modern Art Museum in Paris, and the museums in Toulouse, Albi, Marseilles, Chicago and Helsinki.

Contemporary

Tired of bananas, oranges, pinks and birds? Choose a poster.

Any given poster wants to bring a message to our notice, to publicize products, invite us to entertainments or advertise campaigns. It can inform, stimulate, activate, mobilize, expound, query, provoke, motivate or convince - or all of these things. The task of the poster should not, however, be limited to mere communication between producer and consumer, but should also improve the aesthetic sense of its viewers, thereby making a contribution to their artistic awareness and their awareness of their fellow human beings.

Adolphe-Léon Willette, French, (1857-1926)

Original lithograph from "Les Affiches Illustrées" series

Willette's elegant vertical poster of a traditional Dutch girl in national dress presenting us with Van Houten's cocoa (hot chocolate), set against a wonderful background of gilded leaves. It is both direct and effective in it's design.

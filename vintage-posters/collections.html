Les Maitres de l'Affiche
The Poster Masters was the prestigious publication of a monthly portfolio of poster prints of the greatest posters of the Belle Époque. Selected by one of the early poster chroniclers, Roger Marx, the series appeared between 1896 and 1900. The posters were issued as separate numbered sheets, referred to as plates, "PL.1" to "PL.240."...
Buvard
During the 1950's and 60's in France small advertising posters were printed and distributed to offices, schools, stores, and bars to promote products. These small pieces are well preserved and have the same quality of graphic design and range as their larger counterparts. Small size, big impact!
